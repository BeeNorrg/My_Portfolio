About Me

Brandon Norrgard is a an up-and-coming web designer based out of Roseville, Minnesota. Born in Connecticut, he moved to Minnesota as a child to be with family and has remained ever since. His interests include writing, skateboarding, and role-playing games. He aspires to create his own role-playing game in the future; and use web-integration to make the gaming genre even more accessible to the public at large.
